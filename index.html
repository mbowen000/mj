Accommodations

Chicago has a huge selection of Hotels mainly centered around the downtown area. Due to the business of the holiday, we recommend booking as soon as possible! Please don't hesitate to reach out to Mike or Julia if you have any questions about lodging or anything else!



Since our wedding is happening along with the 4th of July weekend, prices might be a little higher than normal, but we hope you can find something that works. Here are some tips:

  • The downtown loop area doesn't have the lowest prices, but if you're looking to do some sight-seeing then it would be very convenient to stay here. Recommendations include:
    • Central Loop Hotel
    • Hyatt Place River North
  • If you're looking to save some money or are looking to make a quick escape, the hotels near O'hare International Airport have some lower prices.
  • Airbnb is widely available throughout the city and can save some serious money and provide an experience that is usually pretty awesome. Try finding a place near Roscoe Village, West Lakeview, or Lincoln Square and you'll be in a really cool neighborhood. Pro-tip: Get a house with a bunch of friends and split the cost!
